Brand Style Guide for DXB Stag Parties

PRIMARY COLORS:
	•	Gold (Metallic Look):
	•	HEX: #D4AF37
	•	RGB: 212, 175, 55
	•	Use for: logo, headings, CTAs, accents
	•	Optional gradient or bevel overlay for a 3D metallic effect
	•	Deep Indigo/Navy (Background Base):
	•	HEX: #0B0E1C
	•	RGB: 11, 14, 28
	•	Use for: main background, header/footer, banners
	•	Charcoal Black (Alt dark):
	•	HEX: #1A1A1A
	•	Use for: secondary backgrounds, contrast sections
	•	Champagne/Soft Gold (Accent):
	•	HEX: #F7E7CE
	•	For highlights or light backgrounds with a soft luxe feel

TYPOGRAPHY:

Primary Font – Headings & Logo Style:
	•	Font Name: Cinzel or Trajan Pro
	•	Serif font with classical Roman proportions
	•	All-caps for titles and logo use
	•	Matches the luxury, formal tone of the brand

Secondary Font – Body Text:
	•	Font Name: Lora, Georgia, or Playfair Display
	•	Elegant serif font
	•	Use for paragraphs and content blocks
	•	Easy to read while maintaining high-end style

Optional Display Font (for stylised headings or CTAs):
	•	Cormorant Garamond or EB Garamond

⸻

BUTTON STYLE:
	•	Background: Gold (#D4AF37)
	•	Text: White or Navy (#FFFFFF or #0B0E1C)
	•	Hover State: Reverse (Navy background with Gold text or outline)


ICON STYLE:
	•	Use thin-line, gold or champagne-colored icons
	•	Prefer outline over filled to keep it refined
	•	Consistent weight with font sizing

⸻

OTHER NOTES FOR DESIGNER:
	•	Gold accents should feel bold but tasteful, not flashy
	•	Avoid overuse of drop shadows — keep the look crisp
	•	Consider soft indigo gradients as background overlays in content sections to create depth
	•	When using the logo, always preserve spacing around it, and avoid placing it on busy imagery unless a solid navy block is used behind
